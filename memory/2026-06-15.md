### Bug 2: Broken Regex in normalizeSongTitle
- During earlier sed/python edits, backslashes were stripped from regexes:

  - `\(.*?\)` → `(.*?)`

- This caused normalize function to produce garbage output (e.g. `sau lời từ khước []`)
- Fixed: rewrote entire script cleanly with proper regexes
